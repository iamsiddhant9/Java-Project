Core Platform Features

Discover how TiffinConnect delivers guaranteed freshness, flexible ordering, and reliability for both customers and verified providers.

Verified Partners

Every tiffin provider undergoes a rigorous **3-step verification process**, including hygiene audits and initial quality checks, ensuring only the best and most reliable kitchens serve our customers.

Guaranteed Freshness

Providers set a fixed daily limit (between **50 and 100 tiffins**). Once this limit is reached, ordering closes, guaranteeing that every meal is freshly prepared and preventing the use of stored ingredients.

Portion Customization

Customize your order with **full or half portions** based on your hunger and budget. All orders must be finalized before the **daily cutoff time** to allow providers adequate preparation time.

Transparent Ratings

Decide confidently using community-driven ratings. Only verified customers can leave reviews, giving you the most **accurate and honest picture** of a provider's quality and service history.
